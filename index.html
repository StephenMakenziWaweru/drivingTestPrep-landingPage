ABOUT

Inspiration

This project was inspired by some hardships I faced when taking my NTSA driving test. While some driving schools posses some sort of online learning portal, most lack them and those that have are limited in what they can offer. Student's often result to online searching of resources which can be scarce or highly dis-organized. Additionally, at the height of the recent covid-19 pandemic most schools were closed and physical learning discouraged.

This project was mean't to address these gaps and provide free resources to all. It also allows users to improve the site by providing resources they found useful in their journey. It essentially prepares learner drivers to take the test with confidence.

The project was initially done in the two weeks alloted for Portfolio Projects done at Holberton School.
